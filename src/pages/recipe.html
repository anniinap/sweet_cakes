Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 175°C. Grease and flour a springform pan.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients: Combine the flour, starch, cocoa powder, baking powder, vanilla sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Cream the butter and sugar: Beat the room-temperature butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs: Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Incorporate dry ingredients and milk: Gradually add the dry ingredients and milk alternately to the batter, starting and ending with the dry mixture.
  6. Bake: Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 35–40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let the cake cool completely before assembling.

  1. Whip the cream: Beat the heavy cream until soft peaks form.
  2. Mix the cream cheese: In a separate bowl, mix the cream cheese, icing sugar, and lemon juice until smooth.
  3. Combine: Gently fold the cream cheese mixture into the whipped cream until smooth and fluffy.

  1. Slice the cake: Cut the cooled cake horizontally into two or three layers.
  2. Add the filling: Spread a generous amount of the cream cheese filling between each layer, reserving some for the top.
  3. Decorate: Spread the remaining filling over the top of the cake and decorate with blueberries and chopped hazelnuts.

Tips

Make the cake a day ahead to allow the flavors to meld.

Store the cake in the refrigerator until ready to serve.

Enjoy your baking!
